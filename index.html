Hi ! I'm Udit Saxena.

I am a Master's student at the UMass, Amherst studying Computer Science.

My interests lie in the fields of Machine Learning and Distributed Systems and I am particularly excited to work on applications at the intersection of the two (though during grad school, I usually find myself working mostly on projects related to Deep Learning.)

I'm currently an intern in the Bay Area, working at Sumo Logic with the metrics team led by David Andrzejewski.

During Spring 2017, I worked with Prof. Andrew McCallum on serving pre-trained Tensorflow models for the JVM as part of IESL.

I was a Google Summer of Code intern, working with MLPACK and Ryan Curtin, a C++ based open source machine learning library.

I have completed a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics from BITS Pilani. In the past I have interned with Adobe and worked as a Product Engineer with the Core team at Sprinklr.

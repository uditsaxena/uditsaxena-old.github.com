Hi ! I'm Udit Saxena.

I am a Master's student at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst studying Computer Science.

My interests lie in the fields of Machine Learning and Data Science and I am particularly excited to work on applications at the intersection of the two.

I have completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science and my Master's in Mathematics from the Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani.

I have worked as a Product Engineer with the Core team at Sprinklr, a social media analytics startup.

At Sprinklr, I was responsible for REST-based API integrations and extensions, third-party product integrations and implementing Single Sign On with Sprinklr as an Identity Provider.

In the summer of 2014, I was a Google Summer of Code intern, working with MLPACK, a C++ based open source machine learning library. At MLPACK, I worked on including the Adaboost algrithms for the library, by using Decision Trees and a Perceptron (a single layer neural network) as weak learners.
